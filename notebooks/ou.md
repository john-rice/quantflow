## Non-gaussian OU process

Here we model the intensity process as a non-Gaussian Ornstein-Uhlebeck process

\begin{equation}
    d \lambda_t = -\kappa\lambda_t dt + d j_{\kappa t}
\end{equation}

where $j_t$ is a non-decreasing compound Poisson process.
